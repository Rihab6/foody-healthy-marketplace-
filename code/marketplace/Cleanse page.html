CLEANSE

The Cleanse is a short-term nutritious plan designed to help you cleanse and reset. It temporary removes solid food
from your diet and replaces it with fresh juices and light soups all 100% natural, rich in vitamins, anti-oxidants and
plant enzymes, free from additives and preservatives. It is a whole body cleanse focusing on bringing you back to
your natural state of energy and well-being. The menu includes 3 healthy fresh smoothies to be consumed for
breakfast and as a morning and afternoon boosters and 2 light vegan soups to get you through lunch and dinner.
They are all made of fresh fruits and/or vegetables and/or vegan nut milks. The 5 days cleansing can be started every
Monday, and the 3 days cleansing can be started either on Mondays, Tuesdays, and Wednesdays. Please inform us if
you have any specific medical condition or if you are taking any kind of medication before enrolling in this program.
