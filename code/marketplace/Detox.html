Detox

The Detox is a meat-free, dairy-free, and wheat-free diet plan designed for an ultimate cleanse. It is based on the concept of temporarily eliminating inflammatory food and giving your system a break. This helps in reducing chronic and toxic stress while replenishing beneficial gut bacteria for total-body wellness. This plan is rich in super food ingredients, fibers and antioxidants, low in saturated fats and free of any processed ingredients. It is based on grains, pulses, vegetables, fruits and a healthy source of carbohydrates. Protein sources include fish, seafood, beans, nuts and seeds. On a daily basis, you will receive breakfast, lunch, dinner and two snacks based on a creative pesco-vegetarian menu that includes a variety of International, Asian and Oriental cuisine, main course salads, healthy bowls, light wraps, vegan desserts and many more. The diet starts with 2 liquid days (fresh juices, vegan milks and soups). Each week we introduce 1 or 2 liquid days to help the weight loss process. Please inform us if you have any specific medical conditions or if you are taking any kind of medication before enrolling in this program.
