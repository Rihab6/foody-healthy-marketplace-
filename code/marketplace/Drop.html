DROP

The Drop is a nutritionally balanced, low calorie diet plan that has been created for a healthy and long-term weight loss.
It is based on a portion and calorie-controlled menu that includes all food groups and is personalized to meet the lifestyle needs of each client. This meal plan consists of mouth-watering, restaurant-quality recipes that incorporate healthy starches, lean meats, an abundance of vegetables, fresh salads, fruits and low-fat sugar-free desserts. You get to choose your own food from 3 to 5 different options at each meal. All your dislikes and allergies will be taken into consideration.
