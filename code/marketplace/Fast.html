FAST

The Fast diet plan is designed to ensure weight loss through the principle of intermittent fasting, which involves eating
only during an 8-hour window and fasting for the remaining 16 hours. This concept can help you lose weight, improve
your health and simplify your lifestyle. However, it is very important to consume a healthy diet during the eating period.
This plan has been created to guarantee that you are getting all your macro and micro-nutrients along with an adequate
calorie intake to make the best out of your fasting experience. Based on your schedule and convenience, you can choose
between our AM package which includes breakfast, lunch and 2 snacks or our PM package which includes lunch, dinner
and 2 snacks. The menu is based on a low fat healthy version of a variety of International, Asian and Oriental dishes,
main course salads and light sandwiches. It is made out of mouth-watering, restaurant-quality recipes including healthy
starches, lean meats, an abundance of vegetables and fresh salads, fruits and low fat sugar-free desserts. You will get to
choose your own food from 3-5 different options at each meal. All your dislikes and allergies will be taken into
consideration.
Please inform us if you have any specific medical condition or if you are taking any kind of medication before enrolling
in this program.
