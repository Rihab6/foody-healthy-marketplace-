KETO

The Keto is a portion-controlled, very low carbohydrate diet with adequate amounts of proteins and healthy fats.
Throughout the entire diet program, net carbs will be reduced to less than 30g per day, a level to which the body
responds by breaking up fat to release energy and that is the metabolic state of ketosis. The menu includes a Keto
version of a variety of International, Asian and Oriental dishes. It is made of nutritionally designed meals including
delicious full fat dairies, meats, eggs, keto-friendly ingredients, limited amounts of vegetables and an abundance of
healthy fat sources. You will receive Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner and two Snacks on a daily basis. We will carefully
select your menu ensuring you get your entire daily nutrient requirements while consuming a variety of tasty dishes.
You will have the option to make changes to the menu based on your food preferences. Please inform us if you have
any specific medical condition or if you are taking any kind of medication before enrolling in this program.

When to choose and What to Expect :

>
CHOOSE THIS PLAN IF YOU :
  • Prefer, understand and can handle a carb-restricted diet
  • Are willing to fully commit for 14 or 28 consecutive days (consuming none keto-friendly food might get you out of the Ketosis status and compromise your weight-loss results )
  • Are looking to get rid of a few extra stubborn kilos
  • Are looking for a fast efficient yet healthy weight-loss
  • Find it difficult to lose weight following traditional balanced diets
  • Are facing a plateau in your weight loss journey
  • Have no medical conditions
EXPECTED RESULTS :
  • Weight loss (3 to 5 kg in 14 days)
  • Lower percentage of body fat
  • Improved physical shape
