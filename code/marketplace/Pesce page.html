PESCE

The Pesce plan is a calorie controlled diet that is free from chicken and meat. It is based on seafood, grains, pulses,
vegetables, fruits and a healthy source of carbohydrates. Protein sources include dairies, eggs (with a possibility of
removing it), fish, seafood, beans and some soy products. The menu includes a vegetarian version of a variety of
International, Asian and Oriental dishes, main course salads and light sandwiches. You will get to select your own
food from 3 different options at each meal. All your dislikes and allergies will be taken into consideration.

PRICING :







When to choose and What to Expect :

CHOOSE THIS PLAN IF YOU :

  • Are pescatarian by nature and looking to meet your daily nutrient requirements
  • Are trying to switch to a pescatarian diet for ethical, environmental or health reasons
  • Are looking to give your body a break from meat and chicken
  • Love plant-based food, fish and seafood

EXPECTED RESULTS :

  • Weight-loss of 1 to 2% of initial weight per week or weight maintenance based on your target and the calorie intake you select
  • Improved heart health
  • Lower risk of chronic diseases
  • Lower cholesterol and triglyceride levels
  • Controlled blood sugar levels
