PROTEINS

The Proteins plan is a healthy low calorie plan with a low to moderate amount of carbohydrates. It is based on a balanced nutrient dense menu with an average of 30-40% of total calorie intake from carbs and 20-30% from proteins. The menu includes a healthy less carbs version of a variety of International, Asian and Oriental dishes, main course salads and nutritious casseroles. While most of the breakfast options include healthy starches, the carbs of lunch and dinner dishes are often replaced by mouth-watering flavored vegetables (steamed, sauteed, mashed or simply grilled). Snacks include fresh fruits, healthy nuts and some healthy sugar-free desserts. You will get to choose your own food from 3 to 5 different options at each meal. All your dislikes and allergies will be taken into consideration.

PRICING :













When to choose and What to Expect :

CHOOSE THIS PLAN IF YOU ARE :

  • Carbs conscious
  • Looking to lose weight relatively fast, but not through a very restrictive diet.
  • Looking to boost your protein intake without increasing your calorie and fat consumption .

EXPECTED RESULTS :

  • Weight-loss of 1-2 Kg per week
  • Lower cholesterol and triglyceride level
  • Controlled blood sugar levels
  • Improved body shape
