A plant based diet free from meat, dairies and egg.

VEGAN

The Vegan is a calorie controlled diet that is free from all animal products (chicken, meat, fish, eggs and dairies).
It is based on grains, pulses, vegetables, fruits and a healthy source of carbohydrates. It is high in fiber, vitamins and anti-oxidants, low in saturated fat and cholesterol. Protein sources include beans, nuts, tofu and some vegan cheeses. The menu includes a vegan version of a variety of International, Asian and Oriental dishes, main course salads, light wraps and nutritious casseroles. You will get to choose your menu from 2-3 different options at each meal.

When to choose and What to Expect :

CHOOSE THIS PLAN IF YOU :

  • Are vegan by nature and looking to meet your daily requirements
  • Are trying to switch to a vegan diet for ethical, environmental or health reasons
  • Are looking to give your body a break from animal fats
  • Love plant-based dishes

EXPECTED RESULTS :

  • Weight-loss or weight maintenance based on your target and the calorie intake you select
  • Improved heart health
  • Lower risk of chronic diseases
  • Lower cholesterol and triglyceride levels
  • Controlled blood sugar levels
