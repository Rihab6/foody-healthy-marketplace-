VEGGIE

The Veggie plan is a calorie controlled diet that is free from chicken, meat and seafood. It is based on grains, pulses, vegetables, fruits and a healthy source of carbohydrates. Protein sources include dairies, eggs (with a possibility of removing it), beans and some soy products. The menu includes a vegetarian version of a variety of International, Asian and Oriental dishes, main course salads and light sandwiches. You will get to choose your own food from 3 different options at each meal. All your dislikes and allergies will be taken into consideration.

When to choose and What to Expect :

CHOOSE THIS PLAN IF YOU :

  • Are vegetarian by nature and looking to meet your daily nutrient requirements
  • Are trying to switch to a vegetarian diet for ethical, environmental or health reasons
  • Are looking to give your body a break from animal fats
  • Love plant-based dishes and eat dairies

EXPECTED RESULTS :

  • Weight-loss of 1 to 2% of initial weight per week or weight maintenance (based on your target and the calorie intake you select)
  • Improved heart health
  • Lower risk of chronic diseases
  • Helps lower cholesterol and triglyceride level
  • Helps control blood sugar levels
