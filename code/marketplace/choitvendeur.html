To Become A Seller

The products sold must comply with a number of health and safety rules. The rules relating to product temperature must, for example, be observed: packaged products must be stored at the temperature indicated on the packaging and other products at temperatures between 3 and 8 degrees depending on the food. Rules of transparency with regard to the materials used and the prices are also to be respected on the packaging. For each product sold, you must also follow certain rules. Poultry, cheeses, eggs or cold cuts, for example, must meet strict hygiene rules and specific information must be written on the products. Good to know: the Departmental Directorates for the Protection of Populations (DDPP) carry out inspections of health and safety rules in the markets. In particular, they can control personnel, equipment and products.
