Created to help you achieve a healthy and appropriate weight gain during pregnancy and/or breastfeeding while ensuring that your baby’s nutritional needs are met.

MOM

The Mom diet includes two plans: the Mom to be diet (for pregnancy) and the Mama diet (for breastfeeding)
Our moms-to-be plan is based on a varied diet which includes the right amount from each food group to achieve
a healthy and appropriate weight gain while ensuring that both you and your baby’s nutritional needs are met.
Our mama's plan is crafted to help you get back to your usual weight while supplying the nutrients you need
during breastfeeding, such as proteins, calcium, iron and vitamins. Both of these plans are based on a
well-balanced menu that includes controlled portions of healthy starches, lean meats, low fat dairies, sanitized
vegetables/fruits, and healthy fats. The menu contains a low-fat healthy version of a variety of International,
Asian and Oriental dishes, main course salads and light sandwiches. You will receive breakfast, lunch, dinner
and three snacks on a daily basis. We will carefully select your menu ensuring you get your entire daily nutrient
requirements through consuming a variety of tasty dishes, while having the option to make changes to the menu
based on your food preferences. ​

When to choose and What to Expect :

  • Prevention or management of health complications (hypertension, gestational diabetes, etc...)

  • Healthy and safe weight-loss while breastfeeding
